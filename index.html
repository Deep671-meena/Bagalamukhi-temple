About the Temple

Maa Baglamukhi Temple in Nalkheda, Madhya Pradesh, is a powerful Shaktipeeth known for tantric sadhana and spiritual energy. Devotees from across the country come here to perform rituals and seek blessings from Maa Baglamukhi.

Our Services

  • Havan: Personalized fire rituals for protection, prosperity, and spiritual growth.
  • Anushthan: 3 to 11-day spiritual processes conducted by experienced priests for special intentions.
  • Pujan: Daily and special occasion pujas like Baglamukhi Mahapujan, Shatru Vinash Pujan, and more.
